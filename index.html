About Me

Hi, I'm Ishika Agarwal, a passionate fashion and textile designer specializing in creative and innovative designs. My work is inspired by a blend of modern techniques and traditional aesthetics, focusing on sustainability and style. I aim to create pieces that tell a story and leave a lasting impression.

With a background in exploring materials, colors, and textures, my projects range from denim explorations to bespoke garment creations. I invite you to explore my journey through this portfolio.
